Lines of research

Fair NLP

My goal is to make NLP more equitable, mitigating biases and toxic behaviour in language models, and promoting fairness in AI systems. My work explores both intrinsic and extrinsic types of bias to measure and understand fairness in large language models.

Survey on fairness measures Intrinsic and extrinsic bias

Efficient NLP

Efficiency in NLP is a core focus of my research. I aim to develop training methods that require less computational resources and leverage smarter initialization techniques, particularly for low and mid-resource languages.

FairDistillation Tik-to-Tok

Dutch NLP

My ambition is to create a fully conversational agent in my native language, Dutch. This has led to the development of state-of-the-art models like RobBERT and its various derivatives, including distilled variants and an updated version, RobBERT-2023.

RobBERT Dutch humour detection COVID measures on Twitter
