Resource Description License
dbrd-gendered An extension of the Dutch Book Reviews Dataset (DBRD) with the self-reported gender of the review authors. download
RobBERT A Dutch BERT model based on RoBERTa. MIT more info
Dutch professions A list of professions in Dutch with an associated gender, based on Debiaswe. Used for evaluating a gender bias in the RobBERT paper. MIT download
What the mask Dataset on which cities in Flanders require people to wear a mask, with GeoJSON polygons. Used for the 'What the mask' application. download
